Q:what are HTML entities?listout 5 commonly used HTML entities?
A:HTML entities are special codes used in HTML to represent characters and symbols. Here are some commonly used HTML entities:
    i- &lt; - Less than sign (<)
    ii- &gt; - Greater than sign (>)
    iii- &copy; - Copyright symbol (©)
    iv- &euro; - Euro symbol (€)
    v - &amp; - Ampersand (&)
